About Me

My name is Drew Partin. I'm a Computer Science student, a husband, and a father of three. I have served 20 years in the U.S. Marine Corps and have decided to move on and pursue a new career as a software engineer. I am currently attending the Microsoft Software & Systems Academy.

Education

  • Associate of Arts in General Studies from the University of Phoenix
  • Current Senior at Natonal University, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
  • Currently attending the Microsoft Software & Systems Academy

Work Experience

  • Served 20 years in the US Marine Corps as an Aviation Ordnance Systems Technician and Chief
  • 4 Iraq deployments, 3 years as a Marine Combat Instructor, served aboard a Navy Aircraft Carrier
  • Earned multiple qualifications in Quality Assurance, Production Control, Process Improvement, and Supply Chain Management
  • Gained valuable leadership skills that will last a lifetime

Hobbies

I've picked up a lot of hobbies in my lifetime, because I am always looking for something new to learn. I have been white water rafting in Alaska, skydiving from California to North Carolina, and SCUBA diving and snowboarding all over the world. I have laid floor tiles, built wooden furniture, and I can fix just about anything. The hobbies that have stuck with me since I was a child, though, are reading, a fascination with space, and a strong interest in the hardware and software of computers.

Aspirations

As I transition out of a long military career, my mind is set on doing something that can help change the world. With the current advancements in space exploration, artificial intelligence, electric and automated vehicles, and sustainable energy, it is clear that these technologies are the future. I intend to focus my efforts on becoming a part of that development. Whether it is my software-development, problem-solving, or leadership abilities that help me to to do that, I am committed to the lifelong pursuit of learning.
